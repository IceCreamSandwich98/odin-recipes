Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tspb vanilla extract
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. pre heat oven to 425
  2. Mix all dry Ingredients in one bowl
  3. Mix all wet Ingredients in another bowl
  4. add wet Ingredients to dry Ingredients
  5. wisk
  6. add in chocolate chips
  7. scoop small circular cookeis and place on cooking sheet
  8. place sheet into oven for 20 - 25 min
