Chicken Parm Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken
  • 1 cup panko
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsb olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 4 slices of mozzerella

Steps

  1. dip chicken in eggs
  2. dip egg cover chicken in panko
  3. put olive oil in pan, put panko covered chicken in pan
  4. cook till crispy
  5. set oven to 425
  6. layer fryed chicken with 1 mozzeralla slice and a scoop of tomato sauce
  7. cook till cheese melts
