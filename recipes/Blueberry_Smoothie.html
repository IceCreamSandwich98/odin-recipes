Blueberry Smoothie

This smoothie is great for your body, it includes fruits like blueberries, kale, and spinach, as well as protine powder and almond milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup blue berries
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1 scoop protine powder

Process

  1. Put the almond milk in the mixer
  2. add in blue berries
  3. add in spinach
  4. mix throughly
  5. add kale
  6. add protine powder
  7. mix untill desired consistency
